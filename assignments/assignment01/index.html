Assignment 01

Why did I choose to stay in the Computer Information Systems major? Over the summer, I had been contemplating
taking a lateral shift into Computer Science as my declared major, with it seeming to be more intense coding classes.
This prospect intrigued me because I have been very interested in exploring the more complex and intense sides of the
coding world. All of these facts led me to consider the switch as we came into the 2023 semester.

Upon entering the school year, I began paying very close attention to the kinds of classes that I was taking and
the professors that taught them. One of the specific professors had very extensive knowledge of the Computer Information
Systems major, and from what he described, CIS is very hands on while CS is more theory. Knowing that I would rather
be using my skills in physical application than study, my decision became very clear to me. I decided to stay in Computer
Information Systems in order to maintain a fulfilling career for myself.
